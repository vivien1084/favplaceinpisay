SPLASH SECTION

Hi, to whoever is reading this! This website was created solely for our CS3 class in PSHS - Main Campus! That's not to say that it wasn't fun to make, although I can hardly decide that seeing as I (Rain) only helped with the content and not particularly the code. Pisay, both as an environment and an education system, is entirely stressful and hardly leaves its students with room to breathe, for even just a moment, so it's very crucial that we still find ourselves with happy, peaceful places. These in our website just so happen to be those which we favour over others , and its nice to have that; to have something, that at the end of the day we can go back to and find comfort in, even noisy, distracting areas, or sullen, hollow ones. It's in this way that we can appreciate support systems that come in all forms, not just in friend groups or conversations. :D

DETAILS SECTION

Vivien Ercia
1. Dorm

Why?

The dorm isn't my favorite place because of the place itself, but rather because of the people there. It's where I met my friends who, to this day, remain with me, laughing with me during good days, and helping me through bad ones. It's practically like my second home at this point.
2. Oval

Why?

By no means do PE and 1 Mile Run have anything to do with my fondness for the Oval. I like it there because my friends and I have a tradition of walking around it, to talk or simply just to relax. Generally, I just feel happy when we do an 'Oval Walk' even if the day has made me excruciatingly tired.
3. Basketball Court

Why?

Throughout the course of the many sports I've tried, Basketball has been the only one to bring me enjoyment. Even if everyone prefers Frisbee, I just couldn't seem to grow to enjoy it, but even if I only started liking Basketball now, and even if I'm one of the more amateur members of my club, it still makes me happy.
4. Cafeteria

Why?

I miss the old Cafeteria because, in Grade 7, I met and befriended more people there. We'd always do Truth or Dare there, and sometimes our whole section would take up one of the tables to eat together. Also, at the time, there were many concessionaires to choose from, which I miss compared to the lack of food options currently present.
5. Library

Why?

I only recently began going to the library, but I really like it there because of its air conditioning and bean bags. It's very comfortable there, to sleep, to study, and especially so when there aren't many people around. It's my go-to place for lunch or study period.

Rain Santos
1. Library

Why?

The Library is a cold, comfortable place that was the first ever reason for me to feel like I'd be okay in Pisay. As clich√© as it sounds, having books surround me fills me with a peace that I scarcely every experience nowadays. The beanbags provide me with a place to sleep, and the tables provide me with a place to study. It's as perfect a place can get in a school like Pisay, or at the very least, it's as perfect a place can get for someone like me.
2. Kalachuchi Lane

Why?

As someone who considers herself an artist, there's just something intrinsically beautiful to me about the way flowers fall from the trees and onto the ground. Or maybe I watch too much anime. In any case, I just love spending my time at the Kalachuchi lane, to talk with my friends, to sit and think, or just to rest. Even more beautiful is its state at night, with the lamps hanging from the branches illuminating the darkness.
3. 2A Extension

Why?

My friends are as close as I get to family in Pisay, but I was truly surprised to realize one day that a club could mean more than a hobby to me. Every Monday and Wednesday, Invictus holds its annual meeting at the 2A Extension, and the rush of prepping fills me with stress, but by God is the thrill and fun worth it. I've found myself a makeshift little family in Invictus, and I wouldn't trade them for the world.
4. Oval

Why?

Stress is easy to come by in Pisay, but I'm grateful every day that I have friends who help me through it. The Oval has been a place of ritual since 7th grade, since the day that drama started and I needed to talk my feelings out, and from that came the tradition of "Oval Walk", where, if need be, we'd just circle around the oval and talk about anything we wished to let out.
5. Gaz

Why?

The Gazebos of Pisay feel the most wonderful on Sundays, or days where class is suspended, because there's something about having some place all to yourself that makes it all the better. I love getting delivery there, and laughing with my friends, and studying for tests whenever the place is near-empty, because it feels almost homely in a sense.
